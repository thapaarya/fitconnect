Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply browse our services, select your preferred option, choose a time slot, and complete the booking process. You'll receive instant confirmation!

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule bookings up to 24 hours in advance without penalty. Late cancellations may incur fees.

Are virtual sessions available?

Yes! Many of our services offer virtual options. Look for the "Virtual" badge when browsing services.

How do I become a trainer?

We're always looking for qualified trainers! Contact us with your certifications and experience, and we'll guide you through our application process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and e-transfer. Payment is processed securely at the time of booking.

Is there a mobile app?

Our website is fully mobile-optimized and works great on all devices. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!
